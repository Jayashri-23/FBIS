Job Application Process

Steps to Apply for a Job

  1. Search job openings
  2. Prepare your resume
  3. Write a cover letter
  4. Fill out the job application form
  5. Upload required documents
  6. Submit the application
  7. Prepare for interviews if shortlisted.


Required Documents and status

Document Name Required Status
Resume Yes Prepared
Educational Certificates Yes Pending
Skill Certificates Yes Pending
Identity Proof Yes Submitted
