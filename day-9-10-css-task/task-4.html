Readability plays a crucial role in how users consume and understand written content on the web. When text is well spaced, properly aligned, and visually comfortable, readers can focus on the message rather than struggling with eye strain or cluttered formatting. Adjusting the line height allows each line of text to breathe, making long paragraphs easier to scan and follow. Letter spacing subtly improves clarity by preventing characters from appearing cramped, especially on screens of varying sizes. Text alignment, when used thoughtfully, can create a clean and organized appearance that guides the reader naturally from one sentence to the next. By combining these simple CSS properties, designers and developers can significantly enhance the overall reading experience while maintaining a professional and polished layout.
